Example: Write a model for each of the situations described (either linear or exponential). Assume that you begin with 200g of helium:

  1. The helium decreases by 10g per day.
  2. The helium decreases by 10% each day.

Solution

  1. The helium decreases by 10g per day.\[y=200-10x\]
  2. The helium decreases by 10% each day.\[y=200(0.9)^x\]
