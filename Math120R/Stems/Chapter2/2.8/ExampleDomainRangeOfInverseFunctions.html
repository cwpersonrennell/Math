Given the function, answer the questions.

\[ h(x) = \sqrt{x^3-27} \]
  1. What is the domain of \(h(x)\)?
  2. What is the range of \(h(x)\)?
  3. Find \(h^{-1}(x)\)?
  4. What is the domain and range of \(h^{-1}(x)\)?

Solution

    1. \([3,\infty)\)
    2. \([0,\infty)\)

3. \[ \begin{{array}}{{rcl}} y&=&\sqrt{x^3-27}\\ \text{Swap }x\text{ and }y\\ x&=&\sqrt{y^3-27}\\ x^2&=&y^3 - 27\\ x^2+27 &=&y^3\\ \sqrt[3]{x^2+27} &=&y\\ h^{-1}(x) &=&\sqrt[3]{x^2+27} \end{{array}} \]

4. Domain: \([0,\infty)\) Range:\([3,\infty)\)
